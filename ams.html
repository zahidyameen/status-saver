Privacy Policy

Effective date: 30-04-2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AMS ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users ("you") when you use our Android application.

Information We Collect

Our app may request access to the following permissions for functionality:

  • INTERNET: To access online resources and services required by the app.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE & ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To detect network status and optimize connectivity.
  • CAMERA: To scan barcodes and QR codes within the app as required by its features.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: To record audio for features such as voice input or media capture.
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access and save images and documents stored on the device, only when necessary.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To read and write media or other files on the device (used only up to Android 10 for compatibility).
  • USE_BIOMETRIC: To enable biometric authentication for secure access to certain features.

How We Use Your Information

The permissions are used solely to enable core functionalities of the app. We do not collect, store, or share personal data unless explicitly mentioned or required for functionality.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and ensure your privacy is maintained while using the app.
